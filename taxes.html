Payment Methods

ATM

Pay using an Automated Bank Machine

Warning: Penalties apply if rejected by or paid after your financial institution's local cut off time on the due date. These payments will be processed equal to the next payment date.

Financial Institution

Pay via tellers at most banks and credit unions

Warning: Retain your receipt for proof of payment. Ensure your Financial Institution will forward payments by the due date. Payments made on or before the due date but received by the City after the due date will not receive the discount (for the March due date) and penalties will be applied (for the July due date).

In-person

Pay in person at City Hall during regular office hours

After Hours: An after hours letter drop box is located at both entrance doors of City Hall. These drop boxes will be cleared at the end of the due date (at midnight). Payments deposited after midnight are considered late.

Internet/Phone Banking

Pay via financial institution's online telephone banking

Warning: Penalties apply if rejected by or paid after your financial institution's local cut off time on the due date. These payments will be processed equal to the next payment date. Payments must be processed early enough to be received in the Tax Office by the due date, otherwise, the discount will not be applied (March due date) and penalties will be applied (July due date).

Mail

Mail your cheque and stub to the Burnaby Tax Office

Warning: Mail lost or delayed by the Post Office will be subject to Penalties.

PAD

Pay through pre-authorized monthly withdrawal
